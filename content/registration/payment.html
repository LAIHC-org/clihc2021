Registration

Dear attendee,

Please be patient with our registration system :-)

It has a few steps:

  1. Let us know who you want to register
  2. Pay your registration fee
  3. Send us an email with your payment receipt, so we can confirm your registration

We will give you here instructions to follow each of these steps:

  1. Let us know who you want to register
    1. Access the registration system and click on “Register now” (blue button):
    2. On the registration page, you add your information here and then click on “Registrarse” (blue button):
    3. You will get this message, but this is not the end!
  2. Pay your registration fee
    1. Within 15 minutes, you will receive an email from usm@usm.cl. This email describes the registration fees. Please click on the “link” included in the first paragraph.
    2. The link will take you to PayPal to make a payment. Here, you need to choose your registration (author, full or conference-only). We recommend you to pay using PayPal.
    3. You could use your account, create a new one or log in with a one-time code.
    4. Pay with your preferred payment method and once you are done, you will receive this message.
    5. Soon, you will receive an email from PayPal (see example, below).

  3. Send us an email with your payment receipt, so we can confirm your registration
    1. Please forward us an image of this payment receipt to clihc2021@gmail.com

      Note that the system can manage payments with credit cards directly. However, your receipt might not be sent to your email automatically. If you pay by credit card, let us know by sending us an email to clihc2021@gmail.com with information about your name and time of payment. We could find your payment in our system internally.
