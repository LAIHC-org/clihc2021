Registration

The CLIHC’s registration system will be available in October 2021. Before that, we would like to inform our community about the registration fees. As the conference will be held online, the costs of running it are lower than if it were in person, but we still need to cover publication and technical support costs. Thus, we will have different registration fees, as described below:

Author registration: $50 US dollars.

At least one of the authors for each paper should register -under the specific fee for authors - to be able to publish their paper in the ACM digital library. Regarding access to conference events, the author registration will work as a full registration. Authors who need financial support will be able to apply for a 50% reimbursement after registration.

Full registration: conference + co-located events: $10 US dollars

Full registration will give attendees access to all conference events and co-located events. Tutorials have limited capacity; therefore, attendees will need to pre-register to be able to join. Workshop participants will be selected by the workshop organizers.

Conference-only individual registration: $4 US dollars

Conference-only registration will allow attending keynotes, paper sessions, panels, and town hall. However, this kind of registration does not include tutorials or workshops.
